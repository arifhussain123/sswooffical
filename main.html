Siksa Tulba Fund

On 10th January 1986 students of Siksa pving in Karachi established a fund to finance their urgent financial needs.

Siksa Students Federation

To expand its area of function members of Siksa Tulba Funda change its name and named Siksa Students' Federation. Now it was serving its members in a better way than it has been serving.

Siksa Students' Welfare Organization

On 23 rd March 2002 with the special request of Skardu Unit and to get recognized its members changed its name again, since then it has been serving Students of Siksa in Karachi as well as in other Cities of Pakistan and Siksa Village.
