Documents Required for Admission


  • CNIC / NADRA registration form.
  • Domicile / Permanent Resident Certificate.
  • Matric / SSC / O level, Mark sheet.
  • Matric / SSC / O level, Certificate.
  • High School / HSSC / F sc / A level, Mark sheet.
  • High School / HSSC / F sc / A level, Certificate.
  • Hope certificate in case of result is in waiting.
  • Migration Certificate.
  • Passport size pictures with blue/white background.
  • School leaving / Provisional, Certificate.
  • Hafiz Quran certificate in case of student is a Hafiz.
  • Others certificate if any; such as sports, debate and socializing etc.

From past till now students are facing many issuses of documents requirement regarding appearence in different tests. These direction are given here for the students to prepare their documents as given above to admit themselves in different Universities. High School / F sc / O level students are directed to apply and prepare the following documents before the announcement of admission in universities and colleges.

  • O level = Ordinary level
  • A level = Advanced level
  • F.A = Faculty of Arts
  • F Sc = Faculty of Science
  • Matric= SSC level
